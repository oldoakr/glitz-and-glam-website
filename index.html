Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the salon offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of beauty services including waxing, facials, makeup, manicure, pedicure, threading, detan, bleach, head massage, and specialized treatments like Korean Glass Facial and bridal makeup.

How can I book an appointment?

+

You can book an appointment by filling out our booking form on this website, calling us at 8088490262, or visiting our salon at Shop no 7, Amba Bhavani Temple Rd, Shamarajapura, Bengaluru.

What are your operating hours?

+

We are open Monday to Sunday from 10:30 AM to 8:00 PM. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

What brands of products do you use?

+

We use premium brands like RICA for waxing, professional facial products, and organic skincare items. All our products are carefully selected to ensure the best results for your skin.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

+

Please call us at least 2 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. We appreciate your consideration as it helps us serve other customers.

Are your services safe and hygienic?

+

Absolutely! We follow strict hygiene protocols, use disposable items where applicable, sanitize all tools and equipment, and maintain high cleanliness standards throughout our salon.

Do you offer bridal packages?

+

Yes! We offer complete bridal packages including pre-bridal treatments, bridal makeup, hair styling, and mehendi. Contact us for customized bridal packages tailored to your needs.
